About NOVA ABA

NOVA ABA provides behavioral services for children, adolescents, and adults. Our mission is to improve quality of life by teaching social skills, communication, organization skils, workplace readiness, leisure skills, and independent living skills. Our staff is experienced in working with individuals diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder, ADHD, ODD, OCD, and anxiety. We also work with individuals who do not currently have a diagnosis.

All of our services are based in the evidence-based practice of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA). We use data-driven analysis to create interventions for developing generalizable, lasting, and meaningful change.

We are currently in-network with Blue Cross, Optima, and Tricare. We also provide out-of-network and self-pay services. We work with clients in Washington, DC. and the Northern Virginia areas, including Fairfax County, Prince William County, Alexandria City, Arlington County, and Loudoun County. We also provide virtual telehealth services through a secure, HIPAA-compliant platform. Our staff is fluent in English and Russian.

Maria Soldatenkov, BCBA, LBA

Maria is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst, licensed in the state of Virginia. She holds a Bachelors in Psychology and a Masters in Special Education and Applied Behavior Analysis from George Mason University. She also holds a Virginia teaching license in K-12 Special Education.

Her experience includes direct services in the home, school, and community settings. She has also provided extensive consultation to other agencies, as well as staff supervision and training.

Maria enjoys volunteering in her free time and has worked with a local organization for adults with developmental disabilities for over 5 years. She has also served as the Director of Education at the Museum of Science Fiction.
